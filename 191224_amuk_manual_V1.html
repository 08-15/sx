Just study carefully, it is simple, easy to understand and to handle.

Brief functionality summary:

During installation I register all senders which are at that time in your inbox. I assume that this is the community you are in contact with in general. That means, those email addresses are automatically active users and I do not care about them in future at all. If there is a message from a new unregistered sender, I will inform you with a message X-RAY, what is a protected view without functionalities. Now you can make your decision, whether to activate the new sender by return message or just delete that message. If you take no action, that specific new sender can send you one more mail. By sending  a third message, she/he will be blocked upon that action. That is the default process and we call it "xf" (X-factor). You might change it, if requested and thereafter no new sender will be blocked. For that, check for the related command above.

Thanks much for using our services. We make it simple, to protect your account!
